# Sirkulær økonomi i praksis - gjennom industriell symbiose (Circular Economy in Practice - through Industrial Symbiosis)

## Project Funding
In June 2019, Oslofjordfondet allocated 4 million NOK to support FREVAR KF's initiative titled "Sustainable Innovation Through Industrial Symbiosis."

## Research Leadership
Led by Hanne Lerche Raadal at Østfoldforskning (now NORSUS), targeting the Øra industrial zone outside Fredrikstad, Norway.

## Core Objective
Ensure resources in a defined area are utilized as efficiently as possible through increased collaboration between businesses and organizations. The goal is achieving near climate-neutral operations across the industrial zone via enhanced inter-business cooperation.

## Methodology
### Research Activities
- Survey distributed to approximately 30 companies in the Øra Industrial Area
- Data collection on waste streams, residual products, energy consumption, water usage, and water quality specifications
- Identification of two innovation case studies for development with specific businesses
- Development of a resource database solution for efficiency tracking

## Industrial Symbiosis Definition
The approach enables businesses within a geographic area to collaborate on resource sharing - materials, energy, water, and by-products - applying cascade principles for maximum resource efficiency across quality levels.
