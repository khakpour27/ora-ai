# Denofa leder an fremtidens matproduksjon (Denofa Leads Future Food Production)

## Core Business
Denofa AS is a cornerstone enterprise in Fredrikstad that processes soybeans into soyameal, soyaoil, and soyalecithin - ingredients used in food and animal feed production.

## Scale
- Imports approximately 420,000 tons annually
- 80% sourced from Brazil, 20% from Canada
- Uses only non-genetically modified and sustainability-verified soybeans

## Sustainability
- Requires comprehensive documentation from suppliers covering seed type, fertilizers, pesticides, and complete supply chain visibility
- Focus on minimizing food production footprint

## Future Initiatives at Øra

### Vertical Farming (Denofa Agri AS)
- Established May 2021
- Explores vertical agriculture opportunities in the Øra area
- Reduces water consumption by 90-95% compared to conventional farming
- Lowers CO2 emissions
- Increases Norwegian food self-sufficiency

### Alternative Proteins (Pronofa AS)
- Spin-off company investing in sustainable protein sources
- **Insect larvae:** Full-scale production facility planned for Meløy, utilizing waste sludge from Norwegian aquaculture as feed
- **Tunicates:** Filter-feeding organisms that address nutrient pollution while providing omega-3 rich protein (production targeting early 2024)

## Investment Scale
Approximately 1 billion NOK in total projected investment for future initiatives.
