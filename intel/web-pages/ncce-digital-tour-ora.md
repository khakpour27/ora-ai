# Digital Tour of Industrial Symbiosis at Øra

## Project Overview
NCCE, in partnership with NORSUS and 10 member companies, received NOK 200,000 from Fredrikstad Smart & Næringsfond to create digital presentations showcasing circular economy activities at the Øra Business Area.

## Participating Companies
- FREVAR
- Kvitebjørn Bio-El
- Borg Havn IKS
- Metallco Stene AS
- Stene Stål Gjenvinning AS
- Kronos Titan AS
- Sirkel AS
- Batteriretur AS
- Denofa
- End of Waste

## Project Execution
Filming occurred during May and June, with subsequent editing, subtitling, and animation completed in fall. The initiative documents and promotes the area locally, nationally, and internationally, targeting students and schoolchildren, partners, and potential stakeholders.

## Deliverables
The project produced three films of varying lengths:
- 50 seconds
- 7 minutes
- 27 minutes (two versions available in English)

All films are accessible through the NCCE YouTube channel.

## Impact
The tours have been extremely popular and have been requested and shared by various actors every time they are shown, demonstrating strong audience engagement with the circular economy content.
