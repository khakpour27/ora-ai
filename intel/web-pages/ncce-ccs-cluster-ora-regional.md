# CCS-klynga på Øra – og regionalt: Ny industri under etablering
(CCS Cluster at Øra – and Regional: New Industry Under Establishment)

## Overview
The CCS cluster initiative, led by Borg CO2, has been under development since late 2018.
It aims to establish cost-effective small-scale CO2 capture infrastructure across the
Øra industrial area and surrounding region.

## CO2 Capture Potential
**630,000 tonnes of CO2 annually** across three local locations.
Includes significant biogenic CO2 (bio-CCS) volumes from renewable biological sources.

## Strategic Goals
- Reduce costs for carbon capture technology
- Establish viable markets for captured CO2
- Develop sound business models
- Create jobs and green economic growth
- Contribute to regional climate emission reduction by 2030

## Significance for Industrial Symbiosis
CCS represents a future symbiosis layer at Øra — captured CO2 from one company's
emissions could become feedstock for another (CCU), adding a new dimension to the
existing material and energy exchange networks.
