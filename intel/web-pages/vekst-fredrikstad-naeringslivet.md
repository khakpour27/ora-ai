# Samarbeid og kompetanse gir vekst – Næringslivet i Fredrikstad
(Collaboration and Competence Drive Growth – Business in Fredrikstad)

## Overview
Fredrikstad hosts a diverse business community with leading environments spanning
technology to recycling. Strong tradition of industry dating back to 1567 founding,
evolving from timber trade and shipbuilding to modern industries.

## Key Sectors

### Technology & Innovation
Nationally leading tech environments and expertise in engineering consulting services.

### Recycling & Circular Economy
Leading environments in technology and recycling, indicating strong sustainability focus.

### Food Production
Emerging sector with companies like **Pronofa** producing alternative protein products
(Purply, a meat substitute made from sea organisms called tunicates).

### Maritime Services
Companies like Styvehavn qualified as hydrographic surveyors.

### Building & Plastics
Established sectors across the municipality.

## Øra Industrial Area
Features unique access to energy sources (gas and steam), housing multiple industrial
operations. Key facilities include:
- **Fredrikstad Innovation Park** (Fredrikstad Innovasjonspark)
- **Borg Harbor** (strategically positioned near borders and transport networks)

## Growth Recognition
LCA earned "Gasellebedrift" status for five consecutive years (high-growth company award).
