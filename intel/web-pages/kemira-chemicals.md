## History
- 1970s: Kemira moved to the Ora area, applying chemistry expertise
- 1980: Founded as **Ferriklor** — name reflects its origin processing ferric (iron) byproducts from Kronos Titan
- Evolved into Kemira Chemicals AS as part of the Finnish Kemira group
- 40+ years as technology provider for Fredrikstad wastewater treatment

## Products and Services

### Primary Products: Water Treatment Coagulants
- **Iron chloride sulfate (jernklorsulfat):** Main product, produced from Kronos Titan's iron sulfate byproduct
- **Other coagulants:** Including aluminum-based products (aluminium hydroxide storage expanded)
- **Applications:** Municipal water treatment, industrial wastewater, sludge treatment

### Market Position
- **Market leader** in supplying Norway's municipal water and sludge treatment sector
- Supplies chemicals to the majority of municipal water and wastewater treatment facilities in Norway
- Delivers **90,000 tonnes** of products to customers around Norway annually
- Serves the broader Nordic region (Norway, Sweden, Finland)

### Product Applications
- Municipal wastewater treatment (primary market)
- Industrial wastewater treatment
- Drinking water purification
- Sludge dewatering
- Paper production chemicals
- Petroleum production chemicals
- Mining chemicals

## Production Process
- Receives iron sulfate (jernsulfat) as feedstock from neighboring Kronos Titan AS
- Refines/converts iron sulfate into iron chloride sulfate (jernklorsulfat) — a coagulant
- The coagulant causes unwanted substances to precipitate out of water during treatment
- When water and sludge are separated at treatment facilities:
  - Nutrients in sludge are utilized for agriculture
  - Organic material is converted into biogas

## Capacity Expansion (2024-2025)
- **Investment:** New reactor installation + increased aluminium hydroxide storage capacity
- **Timeline:** New capacity operational towards end of 2025
- **Purpose:** Meet growing Nordic market demand; ensure production reliability
- **Context:** Part of broader European expansion strategy following investments in:
  - Goole, UK (2023)
  - Tarragona, Spain (2024)
- **Driver:** Anticipated regulatory-driven demand growth across Europe

## Environmental Profile
- Core business is environmental: produces chemicals that clean water/wastewater
- Circular economy exemplar: transforms industrial byproduct into environmental solution
- Contributes to carbon-neutral wastewater treatment goals for Fredrikstad
- The iron sulfate feedstock from Kronos Titan avoids this material going to waste

## Role in Ora Industrial Symbiosis
- **Key symbiosis partner:** Receives ~24,000 tonnes/year iron sulfate from Kronos Titan
- **Circular flow:** Kronos Titan byproduct -> Kemira processing -> water treatment chemicals -> used at FREVAR wastewater plant (and nationwide)
- **Geographic proximity:** Located at Oraveien 14, directly adjacent to Kronos Titan at Titangata 1
- **Historical integration:** Company was literally founded to process Kronos Titan's byproduct (Ferriklor, 1980)
- Part of the broader Ora circular ecosystem
