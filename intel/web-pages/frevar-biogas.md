# FREVAR KF - Biogas Plant (Biogassanlegg)

## Production Volumes
- **Raw biogas production**: ~4 million m3/year
- **Upgraded biogas (biomethane)**: ~2.5 million m3/year (fuel quality)
- **First profitable year**: 2025 (1.3 million NOK profit)

## Feedstock
- **Total substrate capacity**: 30,000 tonnes/year
- **Packaged substrate**: 6,000 tonnes/year (requires preprocessing)
- **Liquid feedstock**: 24,000 tonnes/year (delivered by bulk vehicles)
- **Sludge intake**: up to 2,000 tonnes/year (homogenized with hot water)

Sources:
- Food waste from food processing industries and households
- Wastewater treatment sludge from Fredrikstad municipality

## Processing Infrastructure
- 4 reception tanks: 200 m3 each
- 3 series-connected digestion reactors: 2,200 m3 + 2,200 m3 + 3,000 m3 = 7,400 m3 total
- Anaerobic thermophilic digestion at up to 70 degC
- Substrate residence time: up to 21 days in digesters

## Upgrading Facility
- Capacity: 400 Nm3/h of compressed, fuel-quality biogas
- Process: Removes moisture and CO2 from raw gas
- Raw gas composition: mainly methane (CH4) and carbon dioxide (CO2)

## Distribution
- Marketed and distributed through **Gasum** (Finnish energy company)
- Used as fuel for buses, waste collection vehicles, and other gas-powered vehicles
- Some gas used by industrial facilities at Oera
- Distribution reaches Norway and Sweden

## Environmental Benefits
- Replaces fossil fuels in transport
- Produces nutrient-rich soil amendment (digestate) for agriculture
- Simultaneous waste management and renewable energy production
