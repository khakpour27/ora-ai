# NCCE - Norsk Senter for Sirkulær Økonomi (Norwegian Center for Circular Economy)

## Organization Overview
NCCE is described as "Norges største klynge for sirkulærøkonomi" (Norway's largest circular economy cluster). It functions as a collaboration platform and driver for the transition to circular economy and green transformation.

## History
- Established in 2017, with a focus on industrial symbiosis related to businesses at the Øra industrial area in Fredrikstad
- NORSUS was involved in the establishment
- Originally known as NSSØ (Norsk Senter for Sirkulær Økonomi)
- Originated from the Øra Industrial Area, where businesses have for decades focused on utilizing waste energy and keeping resources in circulation

## Role as National Competence Center
In 2023, NCCE launched a preliminary project to establish a national competence center for circular economy. Partner network includes 8 major research and educational institutions: Fagskolen i Viken, NIBIO, NMBU, NORCE, NORSUS, OsloMet, SINTEF, USN.

The center is administratively based in Fredrikstad, Norway's designated "circular economy capital" (sirkulærhovedstaden).
