# Ole Jørgen Hanssen – Nestleder i NCCE
(Ole Jørgen Hanssen – Deputy Chairman of NCCE)

## Role
Elected Deputy Chairman of the Norwegian Center for Circular Economy (NCCE) at their
digital annual meeting on March 18, 2021. The board works with administration and a
resource team to develop new business areas based on circular economy.

## Background and Expertise
- Senior Researcher at NORSUS
- Part-time professor of Renewable Energy at NMBU (Norwegian University of Life Sciences),
  Faculty of Environmental Science and Nature Management
- Instrumental in the Øra industrial cluster in Fredrikstad
- Companies at Øra have long focused on waste energy utilization and keeping resources circulating

## Research Projects
Actively participates in two major research collaborations with the Øra cluster:
1. **earthresQue** (research center)
2. **Bærekraftig innovasjon gjennom industriell symbiose** (Sustainable innovation through
   industrial symbiosis, 2019–2024)

## Vision
Sees great opportunities and synergies for NCCE in the future. Aims to involve students
in circular economy work through NCCE.

## Significance
Ole Jørgen Hanssen represents the personal bridge between NORSUS's research capabilities
and the practical industrial symbiosis work at Øra, having been involved since the early
days of the Østfoldforskning era.
