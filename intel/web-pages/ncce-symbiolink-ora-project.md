# SymbioLink Øra

## Project Overview
SymbioLink Øra aims to ensure optimal utilization of available resources (optimal utnyttelse av tilgjengelige ressurser) on the Øra industrial area in Fredrikstad, Norway.

## Timeline
- **Duration:** January 2025 - December 2025
- **Background:** Follows the 2019-2024 study "Bærekraftig innovasjon gjennom industriell symbiose" (Sustainable innovation through industrial symbiosis)

## Primary Goals
The initiative seeks to realize opportunities identified in the predecessor project by:
- Establishing close collaboration among industrial actors
- Creating a coordinating leadership role ("leder på Øra")
- Maximizing resource efficiency through circular economy principles and industrial symbiosis

## Participating Organizations
- FREVAR
- Borg Havn
- Metallco Stene
- SAREN Energi
- Metallco Kabel
- Stene Stål Gjenvinning
- Fredrikstad Fjernvarme
- NG metall
- Visit Fredrikstad & Hvaler

## Key Activities
1. **Leadership Structure:** Establishing a coordinator position reporting to a steering committee comprising industry representatives, municipal, and regional government officials
2. **Knowledge Dissemination:** Planning an industrial event spring 2026 focusing on circular economy and industrial symbiosis

## Governance
- Coordinator position within Fredrikstad Næringsforening (Fredrikstad Business Council)
- Steering committee of industry representatives + municipality + county council
