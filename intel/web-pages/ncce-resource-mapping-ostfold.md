# Resource Mapping for Østfold County Municipality (2024)

## Project Overview
NCCE and NORSUS launched a collaborative initiative in 2024 to examine resource utilization patterns in Østfold. Focuses on identifying inefficiencies in waste and energy management within regional industries.

## Primary Objectives
Map resource flows in the region, with a focus on waste and energy from existing industries. Identify opportunities where businesses can collaborate more effectively and reduce environmental impact through circular economy principles.

## Industrial Symbiosis Focus
Cooperative model where companies exchange surplus materials. Examples:
- Thermal energy discharged from one manufacturer could supply heating needs elsewhere
- Production byproducts become inputs for different processes

## Project Phases

### Phase One
Resource flow mapping utilizing both business data and public information sources

### Phase Two
Potential assessment for symbiosis opportunities and methodology development

### Phase Three
Stakeholder engagement through workshops involving businesses, municipal officials, and community partners

## Expected Outcomes
- Reveal what resources exist in the region
- Establish pathways for more sustainable practices
- Facilitate concrete measures promoting industrial symbiosis and circular economy
- Generate economic advantages alongside environmental gains
