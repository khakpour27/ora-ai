# Forprosjekt Industriell Symbiose Øra
(Pre-Project: Industrial Symbiosis at Øra)

## Overview
This pre-project initiative examined symbiotic relationships among industrial operators
in the Øra region, establishing foundational research for a larger funding application.

## Funding & Duration
- **Amount:** NOK 250,000
- **Period:** September 1, 2018 – March 31, 2019
- **Funder:** Norsk Senter for Sirkulærøkonomi (NCCE)
- **Target program:** BIA program from the Research Council of Norway

## Partners
- Østfoldforskning (co-applicant)
- Metallco Stene Stål
- Kronos Titan
- Borg Harbour (Borg Havn)
- FREVAR

## Project Leadership
- **NORSUS Project Director:** Ole Jørgen Hanssen
- **Researchers:** Mafalda Silva, Hanne Lerche Raadal

## Objectives
The team aimed to develop groundwork for an application regarding a main project,
targeting a subsequent BIA program call focused on sustainable innovation. The research
examined how industrial entities could collaborate through resource and waste exchange
mechanisms (industrial symbiosis).

## Outcome
This pre-project led to the successful main project "Bærekraftig innovasjon gjennom
industriell symbiose" (2019–2024).
