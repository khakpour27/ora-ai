# Norsk Gjenvinning Metall AS (NG Metall / NG Nordic)

## Company Overview
- **Legal name:** Norsk Gjenvinning Metall AS
- **Brand names:** NG Metall (now rebranded to NG Nordic)
- **Org.nr.:** 983 413 161
- **Parent company:** NG Nordic AS (group of 42 companies)
- **Ultimate parent:** Norsk Gjenvinning Group (NG Group)
- **NG Group size:** ~3,400 employees, EUR 1.2 billion revenue
- **NG Group description:** Norway's largest supplier of recycling and environmental services

### Equipment
- **Fragmenting plant (shredder):** Norway's most powerful, processes up to 140 tonnes of metals per hour
- **Distinction:** Largest facility of its kind in the country
- **Daily throughput:** Scrap metal equivalent to ~1,000 cars per day

## Operations
NG Metall at Ora is one of nine NG Nordic facilities across Norway, Sweden, and Denmark. The facility is a major metal recycling hub providing collection and treatment of steel, metals, electronic waste, and cables in national and international markets.

### Materials Accepted/Processed
- **Scrapped vehicles** (end-of-life vehicle processing)
- **Construction materials** from building demolition
- **Industrial waste**
- **Electronic products** (elektronisk avfall / WEEE)
- **Batteries**
- **Steel and metals** (ferrous and non-ferrous)
- **Cables**

### Output
- Recycled metal sold to customers worldwide
- Sorted metal fractions for smelters and steel works

## Transparent Metal Initiative
NG Nordic's signature sustainability program that documents and traces metals through the entire supply chain, covering:
- Human rights and working conditions
- Local environmental impact and emissions
- Full traceability from source to final application
- Distinguishes NG Nordic's commitment to ethical and environmentally compliant recycling

## Symbiosis Connections at Ora
- **Receives metals from Ora-area companies** and broader region
- **Co-located with** other recyclers (Metallco Stene, Stene Stal, Metallco Kabel) on Ora
- **Borg Havn:** Adjacent port for logistics/export of recycled metals
- **Output metals** exported to smelters and steel works internationally
- **Electronic waste processing** feeds into broader NG Group waste management network
- **Battery recycling** (co-exists with Batteriretur at Ora)
- **Vehicle recycling** with free pickup service (current campaign)

## Four Primary Service Areas
1. Steel and metals recycling
2. Electronic waste processing
3. Demolition and project work
4. End-of-life vehicle processing

## Key Context
NG Metall/Nordic is by far the largest metal recycler at Ora by volume and revenue. While Metallco Stene and Stene Stal are significant local operators, NG Metall operates at a national/Nordic scale with its headquarters-level shredding facility located at Ora. The facility's 140 tonnes/hour shredder capacity and 1,000 cars/day throughput make it the dominant metal processing operation in the area.
