# Konferanse: Industriell symbiose - hvorfor, hvordan og innspill til virkemidler

## Event Details
- **Date:** September 11, 2024, 9:00 AM - 4:30 PM
- **Location:** Scandic City, Fredrikstad, Norway

## Conference Structure

### Part 1: Øra Industrial Park Status (9:00 AM - 12:30 PM)
- Presentations on material and energy flows
- Barriers to industrial symbiosis
- Regional strategies
- Speakers from NORSUS, Østfold County Council
- Company presentations: FREVAR, Hydrovolt, Norsk Gjenvinning Metall
- Focus on practical collaboration and waste recovery solutions

### Part 2: National & International Perspectives (12:30 PM - 2:30 PM)
- Swedish and international experiences presented by Mats Eklund from Linköping University
- Presentations from Eydeklyngen
- Government representatives: Ministry of Climate and Environment, KS (Norwegian Association of Local and Regional Authorities), Innovation Norway
- Discussion of policy mechanisms and instruments (virkemidler)

### Part 3: Field Tour (3:00 PM - 4:30 PM)
- Guided bus tour through Øra's industrial symbiosis sites
- Facilities visited: Batteriretur, FREVAR, Hydrovolt, Kronos Titan
- Led by NORSUS

## Target Audience
Businesses, municipalities, regional authorities, and industrial parks interested in circular economy collaboration.
