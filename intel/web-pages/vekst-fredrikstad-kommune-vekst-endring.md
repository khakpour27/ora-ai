# En kommune i vekst og endring
(A Municipality in Growth and Change)

## Population & Size
Fredrikstad, founded in 1567, is Norway's sixth-largest city with over **80,000 residents**.
Combined with neighboring Sarpsborg, the Nedre Glomma region encompasses approximately
**140,000 people** and represents a labor market approaching Trondheim's scale.

## Location & Accessibility
- One hour by car from Oslo
- Located in a county serving as a major European gateway
- Strategic position for business development

## Urban Transformation
The Værste riverfront area, once industrial ruins since 2000, has been converted into a
densely populated zone with employment levels exceeding its 1960s-70s industrial peak.
The former Fredrikstad Mekaniske Verksted site offers 68 hectares available for new projects.

## Industrial Evolution
Space-intensive industry in Fredrikstad has largely relocated from central areas, with
significant operations now concentrated near **Borg Harbor and Fredrikstad Innovasjonspark
on Øra**. This reflects the transformation from manufacturing to knowledge-based sectors.

## Business Development
The municipality actively matches appropriate industries to suitable locations. New
office-based enterprises, shops, and restaurants continue filling converted industrial
spaces, signaling economic diversification and growth momentum.
