# Digital omvisning fra den industrielle symbiosen på Øra
(Digital Tour of Industrial Symbiosis at Øra)

## Project Overview
NCCE collaborated with NORSUS and 10 member companies at Øra to create a digital
presentation of circular economy activities. Funded with NOK 200,000 from
Fredrikstad Smart & Næringsfond.

## Participating Companies and Representatives
1. **FREVAR** – Dir. Fredrik Helstrøm
2. **Kvitebjørn Bio-El** – CEO Rune Dirdal
3. **Borg Havn IKS** – Environmental Chief Charlotte Iversen
4. **Metallco Stene AS** – CEO Michael Haraldsen
5. **Stene Stål Gjenvinning AS** – CEO Øyvind Stene
6. **Kronos Titan AS** – Lab Chief Øystein Ruud
7. **Sirkel AS** – Communications Dir. Gunhild Solberg
8. **Batteriretur AS** – CEO Fredrik Andresen
9. **Denofa** – Factory Chief Torgeir Hjertaker
10. **End of Waste** – CEO Toralf Eklund

## Deliverables
Three films of varying lengths: 50 seconds, 7 minutes, and 27 minutes.
Two versions include English subtitles, available on the NCCE YouTube channel.

## Strategic Focus
Showcase circular value creation at Øra to:
- Attract new actors to the area
- Foster business development
- Create regional job opportunities
- Target students, potential stakeholders, and international audiences
