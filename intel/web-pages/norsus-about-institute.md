# Om NORSUS – Norsk institutt for bærekraftsforskning
(About NORSUS – Norwegian Institute for Sustainability Research)

## Organization Overview
NORSUS is a private not-for-profit research institute dedicated to advancing sustainable
development. Mission: providing knowledge for sustainable societal development through
applied research and methodology development.

## History
- **Founded:** 1988 as Stiftelsen Østfoldforskning (Østfold Research Foundation)
- **Restructured:** 2006, became a limited company
- **Name change:** Later rebranded to NORSUS
- **Basic funding:** Norway's Research Council

## Research Specialization
Life cycle assessment (LCA) methodologies for environmental and resource efficiency
analysis. Has worked with circular economy for over 35 years. Among Europe's leading
research communities in LCA.

## Research Sectors
- Waste resources and food waste
- Biogas and biorefining
- Construction and packaging
- Energy and transport
- Food production
- Furniture, textiles, and plastics
- Process industry
- Ocean and coastal studies
- AI and digitalization for sustainability
