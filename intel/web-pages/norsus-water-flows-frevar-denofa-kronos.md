# Vannstrømmer hos Frevar KF, Denofa AS og Kronos Titan AS på Øra i Fredrikstad
(Water Flows at FREVAR, Denofa, and Kronos Titan at Øra in Fredrikstad)

## Publication Year
2015

## Overview
This report examines opportunities for exchanging water streams among three major companies
located at Øra in Fredrikstad. The project applies industrial ecology principles where
wastewater from one facility can serve as resource material for another.

## Companies Studied

### FREVAR KF
- Manages drinking water treatment and wastewater processing for Fredrikstad municipality
- Handles waste management and energy recovery

### Denofa AS
- Produces soybean meal, soybean oil, and lecithin

### Kronos Titan AS
- Manufactures titanium dioxide

## Water Exchange Objectives
- Reduce total wastewater discharge across the three facilities
- Establish closer cooperation for environmentally and economically viable solutions
- Focus on cooling water demand (which is substantial)

## Data Collection
The project built upon a similar 2000 study, revising data on water streams including:
- Type of water
- Usage purpose
- Volume (m³/year)
- Temperature
- Quality metrics

Findings were compiled in an accompanying Excel file documenting potential exchanges
between facilities.

## Significance
This study represents early industrial ecology research at Øra, demonstrating that
water systems operated more linearly than energy and waste networks — suggesting
room for improvement in water symbiosis.
