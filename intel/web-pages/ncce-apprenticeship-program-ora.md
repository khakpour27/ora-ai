# Ny ordning for lærlinger til Øra (New Apprenticeship Program at Øra)

## Context
Øra industrial park comprises approximately 150 companies creating over 2,000 jobs. NCCE collaborated with businesses, Glemmen vocational school, local government, and NAV to expand trainee opportunities.

## Key Companies in Circular Economy at Øra
- Sirkel Glass
- Stene Stål Gjenvinning (Steel Recycling)
- Batteriretur
- Hydrovolt
- Norsk Gjenvinning (Norwegian Recycling)
- Metallco Kabel

## Program Details
- Initially focused on waste and recycling
- Shifted to production technology apprenticeships (better match for business needs)
- Østfold County and NAV developed modular training structure for adults
- Increased workplace-based learning with close instructor support
- Training began autumn 2024
