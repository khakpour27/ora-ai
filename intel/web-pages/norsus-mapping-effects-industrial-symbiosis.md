# Hvordan kartlegge og hva er effektene av industriell symbiose?
(How to Map and What Are the Effects of Industrial Symbiosis?)

## Event Details
A conference titled "Industrial Symbiosis: Why, How, and Measures" was held on
September 11, 2024, in Fredrikstad, Norway.

## Context
The conference drew from two major research initiatives:
1. **Bærekraftig innovasjon gjennom industriell symbiose** (2019–2024)
2. **Klimasats Øra** projects (Fredrikstad municipality climate initiative)

## Key Focus Areas
- Effects of existing symbiotic relationships
- Opportunities for improvement in industrial symbiosis
- Practical circular solutions within the industrial sector
- Mapped barriers to industrial symbiosis

## Notable Participants
- **Mats Eklund** (Linköping University) – international insights on industrial symbiosis
- **Eydeklyngen** – national experiences from process industry cluster
- **FREVAR** – highlighted as providing circular energy at Øra
- Authority representatives from government

## Discussion Topics
- Panel discussion on strategies for promoting industrial symbiosis
- Examination of mapped barriers to expanded symbiosis
- How to measure and document effects of symbiosis activities

## Significance
This event marked the transition from the research phase (2019–2024) to the
implementation phase (SymbioLink Øra 2025), bringing together researchers,
industry, and policymakers to discuss practical next steps.
