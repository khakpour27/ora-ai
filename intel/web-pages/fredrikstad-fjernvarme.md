## District Heating Network

### Coverage Area
- Fredrikstad city center
- Vaerste
- Seut
- Sellebakk
- Rolvsoy
- Oera
- East side to Sellebakk
- FMV area
- Holmen-Seut
- Trara-Valle

### Energy Delivery
- **Heat delivered**: >88 GWh annually
- **Cooling delivered**: 2 GWh annually
- **Energy source**: 95% recovered waste heat from waste incineration at Oera
- **Primary heat producers**: FREVAR and SAREN Energi Bio-El AS (Kvitebjorn Bio-El)

### Technical System
- Hot water circulates through underground pipe network
- Distributes warmth for:
  - Space heating (radiators)
  - Floor heating
  - Ventilation air warming
  - Domestic hot water
- Operates year-round
- District cooling available in Vaerste area
- Temporary heating solutions for construction projects

### Building Connection Requirements
- New buildings >1,000 m2 within concession area face mandatory connection
- Established by Fredrikstad municipality

## Customer Types

- Municipal and public buildings

## NCCE Membership
- Became NCCE member February 14, 2022
- Expertise in circular economy through waste heat recovery

## Connection to FREVAR
Fredrikstad Fjernvarme was co-founded by FREVAR KF. The district heating is almost exclusively
based on waste heat from the waste incineration facilities at Oera (FREVAR and SAREN/Kvitebjorn).
This makes the district heating network a key distribution channel for FREVAR's waste-to-energy
output, extending the industrial symbiosis benefit to the broader Fredrikstad community.
