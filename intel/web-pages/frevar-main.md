# FREVAR KF - Main Overview

## Core Mission
FREVAR KF owns and operates the municipality's process facilities for:
- Wastewater treatment (avloepsrensing)
- Water production (vannproduksjon)
- Energy recovery (energigjenvinning)
- Waste management (avfallshaandtering)

## Facilities (6 major installations)
1. **Energigjenvinningsanlegg** - Energy recovery plant (waste-to-energy)
2. **Avloepsrenseanlegg** - Wastewater treatment plant
3. **Vannverket** - Water production facility (operating since 1950s)
4. **Biogassanlegg** - Biogas plant
5. **Gjenvinningsstasjon** - Recycling station for households
6. **Deponi** - Landfill

## Certifications
- **ISO 14001** - Environmental Management (valid through 2028)
- **ISO 9001** - Quality Management, applied to water supply (valid through 2028)

## Operating Permits
- AH: Waste facility operations (Oera waste site)
- BA: Organic substrate reception and preprocessing
- FA: Combustion facility operations (quota-regulated greenhouse gas emissions)
- RA: Municipal wastewater discharge

## Current Major Projects
- **FARA** (Fredrikstad Avloeps Rense Anlegg) - New wastewater treatment plant for Fredrikstad and Hvaler, budget 2.3 billion NOK, largest public project in Fredrikstad history, commissioning spring 2026
- **Karbonfangst** (Carbon capture) - CCS on waste incineration
- **Kjaerasen** project

## Financial Results (2025)
- Business operations surplus: >10 million NOK
- Biogas facility profit: 1.3 million NOK (first time profitable ever)
- Self-cost operations: ~12 million NOK deficit
- Landfill operations: ~18 million NOK surplus
- Total business result: 10.7 million NOK (vs ~2 million budgeted)
- FARA project deficit: ~22.5 million NOK (due to interest rate increase)

## Role in Oera Industrial Symbiosis
FREVAR is described as "motoren i energikretsleopet paa Oera" (the engine of the energy cycle at Oera). It is the central energy hub converting waste to steam that powers multiple industrial companies in the Oera industrial area.
