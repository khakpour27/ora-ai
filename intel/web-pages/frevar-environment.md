# FREVAR KF - Environmental Data & Certifications

## Environmental Policy
Integrated HMS, quality, energy, and environmental policy approved by board June 10, 2025.

### Commitments
- Workplace safety and prevention of incidents
- Energy-efficient, innovative solutions for circular economy
- Long-term sustainable facility design and operation
- Circular solution processes
- Responsible procurement practices
- Climate-neutral energy production as industry leader
- Local climate impact reduction
- Transparency and stakeholder dialogue
- Security against terrorism, sabotage, and intrusion

## Certifications
- **ISO 14001** (Environmental Management) - valid through 2028
- **ISO 9001** (Quality Management, water supply) - valid through 2028

## Operating Permits & Licenses
- **AH**: Waste facility operations (Oera waste site)
- **BA**: Organic substrate reception and preprocessing
- **FA**: Combustion facility with 2024 conditions; quota-regulated greenhouse gas emissions
- **RA**: Municipal wastewater discharge (Fredrikstad municipality, updated November 2028)

## CO2 Emissions Context (from NORSUS 2021 report, Oera-wide)
- Energy-related emissions: ~162,000 tonnes CO2-equivalents
- Avoided emissions (waste-based energy replacing natural gas): ~95,000 tonnes CO2-eq
- Net energy emissions: ~67,000 tonnes CO2-eq
- Total avoided emissions (waste replacing virgin materials): ~455,000 tonnes CO2-eq
- Net saved emissions: ~390,000 tonnes CO2-eq
