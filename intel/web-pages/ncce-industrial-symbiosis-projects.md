# NCCE Industrial Symbiosis Projects Overview

## 1. Resource Mapping for Østfold County Municipality (2024)
- **URL:** https://ncce.no/en/projects/ressurskartlegging-for-ostfold-fylkeskommune/
- **Partners:** NCCE, NORSUS, Østfold County Municipality
- **Focus:** Mapping resource flows in Østfold, focusing on waste and energy from existing industries
- **Phases:** Resource flow mapping → Potential assessment → Stakeholder engagement workshops
- **Contact:** Tina Wågønes, CEO, +47 402 45 124

## 2. The Magic Factory (Den Magiske Fabrikken)
- **URL:** https://ncce.no/en/projects/den-magiske-fabrikken/
- **Location:** Rygg Environmental Park near Tønsberg
- **Function:** Converts food waste and manure into biogas and biofertilizer
- **Scale:** In 2017, recycled 106,000 tons of organic materials producing biogas equivalent to 5.1 million liters of diesel
- **Partners:** Vesar, Den Magiske Fabrikken AS, Lindum, Redo Biosolutions, Reklima, NCCE

## 3. Sustainable Innovation Through Industrial Symbiosis (Øra Project, 2019-2024)
- **URL:** https://ncce.no/en/projects/baerekraftig-innovasjon-gjennom-industriell-symbiose/
- **Focus:** Resource database and collaborative solutions across Øra industrial area
- **Funding:** Research Fund for Oslofjord (RFF Oslofjord), 3,954,000 NOK
- **Partners:** FREVAR, NORSUS, Borg Havn IKS, Kronos Titan AS, Metallco Stene AS, NCCE, Fredrikstad Municipality, USN, Eyde Cluster
- **International Partners:** Sotenäs Symbiosis Centre (Sweden), Kalundborg Danish Symbiosis Centre (Denmark)

## 4. EarthresQue: Sustainable Use of Surplus Materials and Waste (2021+)
- **URL:** https://ncce.no/en/projects/earthresque-baerekraftig-bruk-av-overskuddsmasser-og-avfall-i-den-sirkulaere-okonomien/
- **Scale:** Eight-year nationwide initiative, 220 million NOK from Norwegian Research Council
- **Lead:** NMBU (Centre for Research-based Innovation), 33 project partners
- **Partners include:** NCCE, FREVAR, Borregaard AS, Borg Havn, NORSUS, Viken County Municipality
- **Focus:** Managing contaminated materials and landfill utilization

## 5. Borg CO2: CCS Cluster on Øra and Regionally (2017/2018+)
- **URL:** https://ncce.no/en/projects/borg-co2-ccs-klynga-pa-ora-og-regionalt/
- **Scale:** 18 partners, targeting up to 630,000 tons CO2 annually
- **Key Partners:** Norske Skog Saugbrugs, FREVAR, Sarpsborg Avfallsenergi, Kvitebjørn Bio-El, Borregaard, Borg Havn
- **Infrastructure:** CO2 terminal at Borg Havn, transport to Øygarden for storage 2,600m below seabed
- **Economics:** 1,250 NOK per ton captured CO2
- **Jobs:** 30-50 direct; 200+ related positions
- **First collaboration agreement between Northern Lights and an industrial cluster**
- **Project Leadership:** Stormkast Utvikling (Pål Mikkelsen), NCCE (Camilla Brox)
