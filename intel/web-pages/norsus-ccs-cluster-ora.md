# CCS-klynga på Øra – Idé-studie
(CCS Cluster at Øra – Idea Study)

## Publication Details
- **Publication Number:** OR.29.18
- **Year:** 2018
- **Type:** Report
- **Authors:** Hanne Lerche Raadal; Pål Mikkelsen (Stormkast Utvikling AS)

## Overview
An idea study demonstrating significant interest and potential for establishing a CCUS
(Carbon Capture, Utilization, and Storage) cluster at the Øra industrial area.

## Key Objectives
- Develop and test cost-effective capture technology on real flue gas from industrial operations
- Explore sustainable CO2 utilization opportunities
- Pilot small-scale decarbonization through hydrogen production from methane with carbon capture
- Build the future industrial cluster with carbon management
- Create local and regional development aligned with circular economy principles

## CO2 Capture Potential
The cluster has capacity to capture approximately **630,000 tonnes of CO2 annually**
across three local locations, including significant biogenic CO2 (bio-CCS) volumes.

## Strategic Goals
- Reduce costs for carbon capture
- Establish viable markets for captured CO2
- Develop sound business models
- Create jobs and green economic growth
- Contribute to regional climate emission reduction goals by 2030
