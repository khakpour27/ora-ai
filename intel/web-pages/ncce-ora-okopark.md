# Øra Økopark: Kunnskap skaper forståelse (Knowledge Creates Understanding)

## Overview
Øra Økopark is an outdoor learning facility in Fredrikstad that teaches children and youth about recycling, circular systems, and nature. Recently joined NCCE as a member.

## Educational Model (Three-tier progressive curriculum)

### First Level - School Garden (Månegartneriet)
- Students in grades 5-6 participate in ecological cultivation
- Learn food production and preparation

### Second Level - Nature Education
- Grade 7 students explore natural habitats
- Plant and animal identification through sensory engagement

### Third Level - Circular Economy (planned)
- Targeting older students
- Connect sustainable practices with industrial systems

## Scale & Staffing
- Approximately 400 students annually
- Seven part-time staff members
- Funded by Sparebankstiftelsen (three-year operational support)

## Leaders
Fredrik Ellefsen and Torill Frydenlund

## Purpose
Bridge local industry and education, showcase how businesses on Øra contribute to circular systems and build community acceptance of industrial operations.
