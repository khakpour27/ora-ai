# Denofa Energy Use and FREVAR Steam Connection

## Denofa Steam Consumption
- **Annual steam usage**: ~100 GWh
- **Primary sources**: Waste incineration plants FREVAR and Kvitebjorn Bio-El (neighboring facilities)
- **Secondary source**: Denofa's own boiler house

## Denofa Boiler House
- **Electrode boiler**: Primary production for Denofa and neighboring companies
- **Gas boiler**: Operates on liquefied natural gas (LNG) as backup

## Steam Flow Detail (from NORSUS 2021 data / search results)
- FREVAR Energigjenvinning delivers ~62 GWh steam to Denofa Energi
- SAREN Energy BIO-EL delivers ~23 GWh steam to Denofa Energi
- Denofa Energi forwards to Denofa: ~99 GWh
- Denofa Energi forwards to Adesso: ~20 GWh

## Industrial Symbiosis
- Denofa receives steam from waste incineration (replacing fossil fuels)
- Denofa supplies steam to neighboring companies via its boiler house
- Processing soybeans is energy-intensive, making waste-to-energy steam critical

## Context
Denofa AS is a soybean processing company at Oeraveien 15 B, 1630 Gamle Fredrikstad,
producing vegetable oil and soy protein. It is one of the largest energy consumers on Oera,
making the FREVAR steam connection one of the most significant industrial symbiosis flows.
