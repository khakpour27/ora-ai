# NORSUS Report: Oera Industrial Symbiosis & Climate Impact 2021

## Report Details
- **Title**: Oera industriomraade: Industriell symbiose og klimapaavirkning 2021
- **Authors**: NORSUS (Norwegian Institute for Sustainability Research)
- **Financed by**: Regional Research Fund Oslofjordfondet and participating companies
- **Full PDF**: https://norsus.no/wp-content/uploads/OR-17.24-Ora_Industriell-symbiose-og-klimapavirkning-2021.pdf

## Energy Data (2021)

### Total Energy Consumption at Oera
- **Total**: ~782 GWh within the defined Oera area

### Energy Mix Breakdown
| Source | Share | Est. GWh |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Natural gas | 34% | ~266 GWh |
| Waste-based steam | 32% | ~250 GWh |
| Electricity | 27% | ~211 GWh |
| Oil | 4% | ~31 GWh |
| Hot water | 4% | ~31 GWh |

### Waste Incineration
- ~130,000 tonnes of residual waste incinerated (across all Oera facilities)

## Key Companies in Symbiosis Network
- FREVAR KF (waste-to-energy, water, biogas)
- Kronos Titan AS (TiO2 production, iron sulfate byproduct)
- Kemira Chemicals (water treatment chemicals, uses iron sulfate)
- Denofa AS (soybean processing)
- Fredrikstad Fjernvarme AS (district heating)
- SAREN Energi / Kvitebjorn Bio-El (waste-to-energy)
- Metallco Stene AS (metal recycling)
- Gipsgjenvinning / Gyproc (gypsum recycling)
- Adesso (receives steam via Denofa Energi)
- Reichhold (receives small steam volumes)
