# SymbioLink Øra (2025)

## Project Objectives
SymbioLink Øra aims to ensure optimal utilization of available resources in the Øra
industrial area in Fredrikstad. Builds on findings from the preceding "Sustainable
Innovation Through Industrial Symbiosis" study (2019–2024).

The core objective involves establishing tight collaboration led by a coordinating
resource to maximize resource efficiency across the industrial zone.

## Duration and Funding
- **Period:** January 2025 – December 2025
- **Funder:** Østfold County Municipality (fylkeskommune)

## Primary Work Areas

### Leadership Structure
Establishing a dedicated coordinator ("Leader at Øra") to facilitate inter-company
cooperation. This position would operate within Fredrikstad Business Council (Næringsrådet)
while reporting to a governance board comprising:
- Industry representatives
- Municipal officials
- County authorities

### Knowledge Dissemination
Planning an industrial event scheduled for spring 2026 focusing on circular economy
principles and symbiosis practices.

## Significance
This project represents the operational next step after the research phase (2019–2024),
moving from mapping and analysis to establishing permanent coordination infrastructure
for industrial symbiosis at Øra.
