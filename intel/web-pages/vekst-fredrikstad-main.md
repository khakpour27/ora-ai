# Vekst i Fredrikstad – Main Page

## Mission & Purpose
Vekst i Fredrikstad works to strengthen business development through collaboration,
establishment, and growth initiatives in Fredrikstad and the Østfold region.
Focus areas: kompetanse – innovasjon – samarbeid (competence – innovation – collaboration).

## Services & Activities
- **Business support:** Guidance on company establishment and development
- **Networking:** Connection opportunities among regional businesses
- **Commercial property services:** Information about available business premises
- **Recruitment assistance:** Resources for job seekers and employers
- **Housing information:** Links to residential property listings

## Content Areas
Regular articles on:
- Innovation and startups
- Technology adoption (AI, cybersecurity)
- Sustainable practices
- Talent development
- Business growth strategies

## Circular Economy Connection
Features coverage of circular economy initiatives, including the national competency
center for circular economy (NCCE), reflecting regional commitment to sustainability.

## Relevance to Øra Project
Vekst i Fredrikstad / Fredrikstad Næringsråd is the organization designated to host
the SymbioLink Øra coordinator role. The Business Council serves as the organizational
home for the "Leader at Øra" position that will facilitate inter-company cooperation
in the industrial symbiosis network.
