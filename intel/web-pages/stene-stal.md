## Operations
Stene Stal Gjenvinning specializes in recycling and sale of steel and metals. The company receives steel, metals, and scrap cars at their Ora facility, and has extensive experience in demolishing steel constructions.

### Process
1. Receipt of scrap metal, vehicles, industrial waste
2. Sorting and treatment
3. Cutting, pressing, and grinding
4. Fragments sorted (material composition analysis: paint types, chromium, nickel, manganese content)
5. Recycled resources delivered to foundries, smelters, and steel works

### Materials Processed
- **Iron scrap** (primary focus)
- **Ferrous metals** (steel)
- **Non-ferrous metals** (aluminum, copper)
- **Scrap vehicles**
- **Steel constructions** (demolition)
- **Industrial waste materials**
- **Output:** Processed iron scrap, sorted metal fractions
- **Byproduct downstream:** Jernsulfat (iron sulfate) for wastewater treatment (post-smelting)

## Volumes
- **Annual throughput:** ~50,000 tonnes of recycled material
- **Growth:** Receiving decommissioned Oslo trams for recycling (significant new contract)

1. **Metallco Stene** - Metal shredding
2. **Metallco Kabel** - Cable granulation
3. **Hafsil AS** - Smelting works (partnership with Nordox)
4. **NorSer Recycling** - Service mechanics
5. **Indre Ostfold Gjenvinning** - Waste management

## Symbiosis Connections
- **Jotul ASA:** All Norwegian-made Jotul wood stoves contain exclusively iron scrap from Stene Stal Gjenvinning
- **Kronos Titan AS:** Receives ultra-pure iron scrap meeting strict quality standards
- **Ulefos Jernvaerk KS:** Customer (foundry)
- **Hafsil AS:** Customer (smelting works)
- **Celsa Armeringsstal:** Customer (steel works)
- **Metallco Stene:** Co-located metal shredding (shared ownership)
- **Metallco Kabel:** Cable recycling (shared ownership)
- **FREVAR:** Sought to lease 10-15 hectares from FREVAR for expansion (denied; FREVAR needs land for landfill)
- **Lithium batteries:** Increasing intake requires enhanced safety infrastructure

## Environmental Impact
- CO2 savings per tonne recycled:
  - Iron: 1.0-1.3 tonnes CO2 saved
  - Aluminum: ~10 tonnes CO2 saved
  - Copper: ~20 tonnes CO2 saved
- Metals can be recycled infinitely without quality loss

## Expansion Needs
- Seeking larger storage space on Ora
- Need to build fire lanes (lithium battery safety)
- FREVAR denied land lease request (landfill is FREVAR's primary revenue, 30M NOK surplus in 2023)

## Sponsorships
